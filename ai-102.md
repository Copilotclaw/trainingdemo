## 🔑 Credentials

Each notebook supports three ways to supply your Azure credentials:

- **Option A** — paste directly into the notebook cell *(simplest for classroom use)*
- **Option B** — [Colab Secrets](https://medium.com/@parthdasawant/how-to-use-secrets-in-google-colab-450c38e3ec75) (`userdata.get(...)`) *(recommended for reuse — keys never saved in .ipynb)*
- **Option C** — upload a `.env` file *(same as the original lab setup)*

> 💡 For classroom use, **Option A** is easiest: paste once, run, clear before sharing.
